The Challenge

Smallholder farmers produce most of Nigeria’s food—but work with limited data, unpredictable weather, pest risk, and high post-harvest losses (up to 50% for perishables like tomatoes). Inputs are wasted. Yields stay low. Income is unstable.

Our Approach

Fusion AgriTech combines data-driven crop tracking with a zero-waste farming model. Farmers get timely guidance on planting, soil care, pest alerts, and harvest windows—while farm and crop waste become organic inputs or value-added products.

What Farmers Get

📅 Crop Calendar

Localized planting & harvest timing with reminders.

🌦 Weather & Soil Tips

Guidance tied to regional conditions—no guesswork.

🐛 Pest Risk Alerts

Reduce losses with early warnings & treatment guides.

🔄 Zero-Waste Toolkit
